# Highly Toxic
Grapes, raisins, sultanas, and currants are **extremely dangerous** to dogs. The exact toxic substance is still unknown, but ingestion can lead to acute kidney failure.

## Symptoms
* Vomiting (often within a few hours)
* Lethargy and weakness
* Loss of appetite
* Abdominal pain
* Dehydration

**This is a medical emergency. Contact your vet immediately if your dog eats even one grape.**
